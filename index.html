Things to do in Uvita

Uvita National Park Nature Guided Tour

Explore one of the most diverse habitats in the world on a guided nature hike through Uvita National Park.

Book Now

Whales watching Tour

You will enjoy a tour of the first marine park in Costa Rica, the Ballena Marine Park in the South Pacific, where the big humpback whales are dating, as well as dolphins, turtles and more.

Book Now

Uvita Rain Forest Night Walk

See the nocturnal side of Uvita Forest on a guided hike, searching for the animals that come out at night. From grey foxes to spiders and reptiles, much of Uvita's wildlife can only be spotted after dark.

Book Now

3 Day Osa jungle Adventure tour

This is a tour designed for adventure and wildlife lovers. Through this amazing journey, you will discover the most incredible trails of Costa Rica, swim in waterfalls, navigate in the Sierpe river under the gaze of crocodiles, find tons of wildlife, sleep in hidden lodges.
